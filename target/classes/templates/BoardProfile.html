Anna Miti Chairperson

Anna Miti is a development practitioner and media trainer with a long background in broadcast media. She is a former AVAC advocacy fellow and an IAS Advocacy for Cure Fellow and grantee.She writes health and science articles and blogs for different platforms.


Catherine Murombedzi

Catherine Murombedzi, nee Mwauyakufa, is the Vice Chairman of the Health Communicators Forum, (HCF). She is a veteran health and development journalist with experience spanning over two decades. She sits on several boards in the health sector and is an award winning writer. She is a Fellow of CNS Health. Catherine is open to learning at every given opportunity.


Michael Gwarisa

Michael Gwarisa is the Secretary General for the Health Communicators Forum (HCF). Michael is a multi-award winning health Journalist and editor for the HealthTimes, a leading online health news publication in Zimbabwe.


Itai Muzondo

Robin Nyembezi Muchetu

About

The Health Communicators Forum is a network of Journalists, communicators and other media persons with a keen interest or directly work in health information. Our aim is to be the bridge between journalists, researchers, communicators, and the community. We sincerely believe that health is more than a news story, but a matter of life and death for everyone. Our members are active journalists. Bloggers and news writers from diverse backgrounds
